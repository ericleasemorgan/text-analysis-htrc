<h1>HathiTrust Main Menu</h1>
<p>This is a text mining interface to sets of personalized "libraries" from the HathiTrust. After enumerating a set of items from the HathiTrust's research center, this tool enables you to cache the set locally, do bits of textual analysis against the result, and provides a means to do "distant reading" against your corpora. A menu of options follows:</p>
<ol>
<li>explore sample collections: <a href="./austen/">Austen</a>, <a href="./channing/">Channing</a>, <a href="./emerson/">Emerson</a>, <a href="./thoreau/">Thoreau</a></li>
<li>after creating a set of items based on the content of the <a href="https://sharc.hathitrust.org">HathiTrust</a>, <a href="./bin/initialize-collection.cgi">initialize</a> your own collection</li>
<li><a href="./bin/list-collections.cgi">list and use</a> the collections you have previously created</li>
</ol>
